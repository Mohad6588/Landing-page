Gaz (candy)

Persian Nougat

Gaz (Persian:⁄Øÿ≤) is an Iranian nougat that originated in Isfahan region. It is widely known as Persian Nougat in American and European countries. It is made from pistachio, almond kernels, rose-water, egg whites and sap from a special plant.
Typically gaz is accompanied by tea or sherbet and is eaten as a dessert after a main course. It is also traditionally served on festive occasions such as Nowruz, the Persian New Year.
